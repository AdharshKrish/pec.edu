Pondicherry Engineering College (PEC) is an Institution sponsored by the Union Territory of Pondicherry, India. The College was started in 1984 under the VII Five Year Plan. It is an autonomous Institution for the purposes of administration, staff recruitment and college development and is managed by a Board of Governors.

Overview

HOD's Message

Dr. Srinivasan G.

The department of Chemical Engineering was established in 2000 with a mission to impart quality education to serve the needs of the industries and society. The department is the sixth Chemical Engineering Department in the government /government aided sector in the region of Tamil Nadu and Puducherry.
The department was started with an annual intake of 30 students in the first year and 5 students in the second year through the lateral entry scheme. Currently the department has an annual intake of 60 students in the first year and 5 students in the second year through the lateral entry scheme.

VISION

  • To become a department that imparts high quality education to its graduates and prepares them to be leaders in Chemical Engineering and allied fields.

MISSION

  • To provide strong technical foundation int the field of chemical engineering.

  • To prepare the students coming from different economic levels for a successful career in chemical and allied engineering fields.

  • To make professional leaders, academicians and engineers with highest moral values and ethics.

Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

PEO1: To Provide Necessary Background In Science, Particularly In Advanced Mathematics, Physics And Chemistry That Underlie Modern Chemical Engineering and Technology.
PEO2: To provide training to solve problems relevant to the general practice of Chemical Engineering and Engineering design.
PE03: To provide students experience in conducting and in planning experiments in modern Engineering laboratory including experiments with computers as well as interpreting the significance of resulting data and properly reporting results in well written technical reports.
PE04: To provide students opportunity to work as part of team on projects pertaining to core and advanced areas of chemical engineering and fostering of important job related skills such as improved oral and written communications.
PE05: To promote student awareness of life-long learning and to introduce them to professional ethics and codes of professional practice.

STUDENT ACTIVITIES (ACES- STUDENT BODY)


  • The Chemical Department has an Association of Chemical Engineering Students popularly known as ACES. Primarily a student body, ACES facilities carrying out of a variety of activities throughout the academic year. ACES arranges various technical lectures by experts from the industries and academia.

  • Once in every three year, a national level technical students’ symposium “TIELINES” is conducted by the department. ACES actively involves the students in various societal activities like arranging seminars, symposiums, national and international conferences to create a platform for the students to exhibit their technical talent and also gain knowledge from participants and experts from other institutions and industries.
