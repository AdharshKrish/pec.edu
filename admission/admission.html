Pondicherry Engineering College (PEC) is an Institution sponsored by the Union Territory of Pondicherry, India. The College was started in 1984 under the VII Five Year Plan. It is an autonomous Institution for the purposes of administration, staff recruitment and College development and is managed by a Board of Governors.

CENTEC

Admission to PEC is primarily done through the Centralised Admission Committee (CENTAC), Govt. of Puducherry. More information on seat allocation and updated course intake can be found in their official site.
*Entry through Lateral Entry is also done through the CENTAC office.

http://centacpuducherry.in/non_neet/

JoSAA

Admission to PEC UG courses can also be done through the Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA), Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD), Govt. of India in a Self-funding quota. More information on seat allocation and updated course intake can be found in their official site.
