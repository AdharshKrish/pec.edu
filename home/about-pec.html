Pondicherry Engineering College (PEC) is an Institution sponsored by the Union Territory of Pondicherry, India. The College was started in 1984 under the VII Five Year Plan. It is an autonomous Institution for the purposes of administration, staff recruitment and college development and is managed by a Board of Governors.

OUR VISION
To foster prosperity through technology by means of education, innovation and collaborative research and emerge as a world-class technical institution.
OUR MISSION
• To create and disseminate knowledge for the betterment of mankind in general and rural masses in particular.
• To impart high quality training to students so as to provide human resource appropriate to the local and national needs.
• To establish Centres of Excellence in collaboration with industries, research laboratories and other agencies to meet the changing needs of society.

NIRF Ranking
The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) is a methodology approved by the MHRD, Govt. of India to rank institutions across the country. The Core Committee set up by the MHRD for NIRF ranking draws an understanding from the overall recommendations regarding each institution in India, based on various parameters. The parameters broadly cover Teaching, Learning and Resources, Research and Professional Practices, Graduation Outcomes, Outreach and Inclusivity and Perception. PEC was endowed with 76th position in the All India NIRF ranking for the year 2019.

Pondicherry Engineering College (PEC) is an institution sponsored by the Union Territory of Pondicherry, India. The College was started in 1984 under the VII Five Year Plan. It is an autonomous Institution for the purposes of administration, staff recruitment and college development and is managed by a Board of Governors. The College is affiliated to Pondicherry University, Pondicherry, for academic purposes. The college campus is situated in Pillaichavadi on the shores of Bay of Bengal, about 130 kms south of Chennai. The magnificent lush green premise along with the neatly laden roads spreads over an area of 210 acres. The highly equipped departments buildings accommodate state-of-art technical and hygiene facilities. The well-lit, airy and spacious administrative building offers a fantastic view of the sea from its many non-glazed openings in the upper floors.
