Pondicherry Engineering College (PEC) is an Institution sponsored by the Union Territory of Pondicherry, India. The College was started in 1984 under the VII Five Year Plan. It is an autonomous Institution for the purposes of administration, staff recruitment and College development and is managed by a Board of Governors.

About PEC

Started in 1984 under the VII Five Year Plan, PEC has been growing from strength to strength and continues to encourage its students to develop leadership qualities and emerge as visionaries making a considerable difference across the globe. PEC is ranked 76 in the Engineering Category by NIRF India Rankings 2019.

Academics

We are committed to providing knowledge and skill development for furthering societal, environmental and organizational growth. There are 12 departments at PEC constituting of Engineering, Science and Humanities disciplines. These departments offer B.Tech., M.Tech., PhD and MCA (only Dept. of CSE) degree courses.

Campus Life

The campus provides a stimulating ambience that motivates students to discover their personal identity and make prudent life choices. Cultural diversity enhances the richness of experience for the students and the campus becomes a powerhouse of inclusion, nurturing expansive thinking, scientific temperament and innovative ideas. In addition to academic pursuits, the campus is a hub for various extracurricular activities and several events under the aegis of various clubs and committees are held all round the year.

Placements

At PEC, every student is important. The Training and Placement Centre strives towards getting industry keen Pecians placed in well-established companies. Many popular MNCs, Tech giants and Core companies visit the campus every year and the Alumni of PEC are spread across the globe occupying policy and decision-making positions in renowned companies.

Admission

Admission to PEC is done through the Centralised Admission Committee (CENTAC), Govt. of Puducherry and the Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA), Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD), Govt. of India. More details on the admission procedure and intake can be found on these websites.
