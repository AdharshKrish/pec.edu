Pondicherry Engineering College (PEC) is an Institution sponsored by the Union Territory of Pondicherry, India. The College was started in 1984 under the VII Five Year Plan. It is an autonomous Institution for the purposes of administration, staff recruitment and college development and is managed by a Board of Governors.

Overview

HOD's Message

Dr. Jayakumar J.

Department of Mathematics is one of the prestigious departments of the Pondicherry Engineering College, established in 1985, with adequate and fully qualified faculty strength. It extends teaching support to all the undergraduate, post graduate engineering and science courses offered by the college besides offering Ph.D. Programme in full time and part-time. It regularly updates the existing courses besides following proactive approach in introducing and offering innovative & application-oriented courses depending on the requirements of various engineering departments to face the ever-growing engineering challenges in the practical world.
It provides consulting services to the students of all the branches of engineering to carry out their projects which involve mathematical applications. The Department of Mathematics of PEC is one of the most popular department for carrying out doctoral research in mathematics.
