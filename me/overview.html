Pondicherry Engineering College (PEC) is an Institution sponsored by the Union Territory of Pondicherry, India. The College was started in 1984 under the VII Five Year Plan. It is an autonomous Institution for the purposes of administration, staff recruitment and college development and is managed by a Board of Governors.

HOD's Message

Dr. N. Alagumurthi

A warm welcome to the Department of Mechanical Engineering, Pondicherry Engineering College, Puducherry. This department was started in the year of 1985. We have a strong undergraduate program in Mechanical Engineering and at the postgraduate level we offer M.Tech in Energy Technology and M.Tech in Product Design and Manufacturing. We also offer Ph.D degree in Mechanical Engineering. The sanctioned studentsâ€™ strength of B.Tech., is 120. The sanctioned strength of M.Tech (Energy Technology) and M.Tech (Product Design and manufacturing) is 17 and 18 respectively.

The curriculum for the students pursuing mechanical engineering is evolved to match the present global scenario and industrial requirements. The primary focus of the curriculum is to equip the students with the latest technological trends to enhance their skills and employability. The courses are designed to empower the students with professional ethics, management skills, knowledge to address societal, environmental and sustainable issues and the ability of independent lifelong learning of technological changes.
It is worth to mention that the Minor and Honour courses in the curriculum will equip the students to function in a multidisciplinary environment by being able to associate and integrate their domain knowledge with other disciplines. The syllabus is periodically revised to update the students with the latest developments in science and technology. Students are encouraged to undertake internship and project work in leading industries and research institutions. We are happy to share that many alumni of this department are employed in leading international and national organizations/institutions of eminence.

Faculty members of the department are highly qualified, experienced and self-motivated. They serve as members of editorial boards and reviewers of national and international journals. Periodically, they organize international/national conferences, summer/winter schools and workshops.
